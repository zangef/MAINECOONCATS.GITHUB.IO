MAINECOON CATS

I started this site as a project after our family’s Maine Coon mix of 9 years crossed over “the rainbow bridge“. His name was Ernie, and he was the most gentle, loving, beautiful and funny cat I’ve ever had. Ever since, I’ve been in love with the Maine Coon breed and learning all I can about them. I built this site to share what I’ve picked up over the last few years from reading and talking to others. Ultimately, I’d like this site to convince as many people as possible that they should adopt their own Maine Coon! To help you decide you could check these cute maine coon cat pictures.
